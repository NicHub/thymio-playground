## Hardware

Microcontroleur : Microchip PIC24FJ128GB106

### INPUTS

- 1 × thermistor
- 1 × IR RC5 receiver
- 5 × front IR sensor
- 2 × back IR sensor
- 2 × ground IR sensor
- 5 × capacitive sensor
- 1 × micro SD memory card slot
- 1 × 3-axis accelerometer
- 1 × microphone
